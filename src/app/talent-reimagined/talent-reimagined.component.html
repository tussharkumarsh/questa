From the Founder

Progressive cultures are usually productive places of work, where businesses have outpaced the disadvantages of failing recruitment practices, and crafting employee-centric retention strategies. The one differentiating factor that these organizations have is to understand the innate motivation, and belief system of individuals and align them to the right role.

We created Questa to empower people to take on the journey of self-awareness and transformation by helping them understand their motivation and behavior with new depth & and clarity. We believe that every individual possesses unique strengths and abilities, and by leveraging data-driven insights, we aim to facilitate informed decision-making processes that drive personal and professional growth. Our organization is committed to providing innovative and reliable Enneagram-backed assessments and services to enable CEOs and senior HR leaders to Reimagine Talent.
